Privacy Policy

**1. What Info We Grab**

We might grab these kinds of info:

a. Stuff You Give Us

*   **Account Stuff:** Like your name, email, phone number, password, and where you get your mail.
*   **Payment Info:** Your card numbers and billing stuff (don't worry, it goes through secure payment companies).
*   **What You Say:** Any messages, questions, or feedback you send.
*   **Extra Stuff:** Things like where you're from, survey answers, and what you prefer.

b. Info We Snag Automatically

*   **Device Info:** Your IP address, what browser you use, your system, and device IDs.
*   **How You Use Our Stuff:** What pages you look at, how long you stay, where you came from, and what you click.
*   **Cookies:** These little files help us keep track of what you like and how you use the site.

c. Info From Other Places

We might get info from:

*   Social media if you log in that way (like with Google, Facebook, or Apple).
*   Payment companies, data companies, and marketing partners.
*   Public records or business partners.

**2. What We Do With Your Info**

We might use your info to:

*   Keep our services running smoothly.
*   Process your payments.
*   Manage your account and chat with you.
*   Send updates and deals (only if you say it's cool).
*   Make the website work better.
*   Stop scams and keep things safe.
*   Follow the rules and laws.
*   Run reports.

We only use your info if we have a good reason, like if you agree, if it's part of our contract, or if it helps our business.

**3. Sharing Your Info**

We don't sell your info. We only share it if:

*   We need to with companies that help us out (like payment processors, IT folks, cloud storage, data analysis, and email).
*   Our sister companies need it for internal stuff.
*   The law says we have to.
*   We sell the company (merger, buyout, etc.).
*   You say it's okay.

Everyone we share with has to keep your info safe.

**4. Cookies**

We use cookies to:

*   Remember what you like.
*   Make things work better.
*   See how people use our site.
*   Show you ads that might interest you (if we're allowed).

You can turn off cookies in your browser settings, but some things might not work right if you do.

**5. How Long We Keep Your Info**

We only hold onto your info as long as we need it to do what we said in this policy, or as long as the law says we have to. After that, we delete it or make it anonymous.

**6. Keeping Your Info Safe**

We do our best to protect your info with:

*   Encryption for sensitive stuff.
*   Secure storage and transfer.
*   Limited access.
*   Regular checkups.

Even though we work hard to protect your info, nothing online is 100% secure.

**7. What You Can Do**

Depending on where you live, you might be able to:

*   See what info we have about you.
*   Fix any errors.
*   Ask us to delete your info.
*   Tell us to stop using your info.
*   Take back your permission.
*   Get your info in a format you can take somewhere else.

**8. Where Your Info Goes**

Your info might end up in other countries. If that happens, we use things like:

*   Standard contracts.
*   Rules from the government.
*   Company rules.

**9. Kids' Privacy**

We don't knowingly collect info from kids under 13. If we find out we have a kid's info, we'll delete it ASAP.

**10. Links to Other Sites**

Our site might have links to other websites, but we're not in charge of their privacy stuff. Check out their policies.

**11. Policy Updates**

We might update this privacy policy from time to time. We'll post the changes here with a new Last Updated date. If it's a big change, we might tell you directly.
