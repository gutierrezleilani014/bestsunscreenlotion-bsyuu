About.

We're a company that's all about sunlight – like, how it makes us feel good and keeps us going. We want to protect everyone who loves hanging out in the sun. It's not something to be scared of, but you gotta be smart about it. The sun gives us energy, helps things grow, and makes us happy, but you need to be careful and take care of yourself. Our goal is to help everyone enjoy the sun safely, so we make sunscreen that keeps your skin and the earth healthy.

🌤 How We Got Started

So, we noticed that people dig the sun but don't always get it. Some people think sunscreen is gross and annoying, instead of seeing it as something good to do for themselves every day. Others just skip it and don't realize how much damage the sun can do over time. Getting too much sun without protection can mess up your skin, make you age faster, and even cause cancer. We wanted to change people's minds and make sunscreen a part of their daily routine that they actually enjoy.

The people who came up with this idea are from all sorts of backgrounds – like skin doctors, scientists, and people who know a lot about skincare. They thought that sunscreen should be both good at protecting you and good for the planet, so they decided to make a new kind of sunscreen that people would actually like using and feel good about putting on every day.

🧴 What We Do for Your Skin

Your skin is like a shield that keeps everything inside you safe. Every day, it deals with pollution, stress, and the sun's rays. We want to help make that shield stronger. That's why our sunscreens protect you from all types of sun rays – the ones that age you and the ones that burn you.

But that's not all. We think about how sunscreen feels, smells, and how it helps your skin in the long run. We mix stuff that blocks the sun with good stuff for your skin, like vitamins and plant stuff, to keep it healthy. We want our sunscreen to protect your skin now and keep it healthy later.

Everything we make is tested by skin doctors. We try to make sunscreen that won't clog your pores, doesn't cause allergies, and doesn't have strong smells, so it's good for people with sensitive skin, kids, and people who get skin problems easily. We think everyone should be safe and comfortable.

🌿 How Science and Nature Work Together

We get ideas from science and nature. Science helps us understand how sunlight affects your skin, and nature shows us how plants can help protect it.

Our labs are where we try new things and still respect old ideas. We check out how stuff like zinc and titanium can block the sun without being too obvious on your skin. We also look into new kinds of filters that soak up the sun's rays without causing problems.

We try to make everything balanced – not just the ingredients, but how well it works and why we're doing it. We don't just follow what's popular; we look for what's true, based on tests and what people say about our products.

🌍 Taking Care of the Earth

We need to protect more than just people – we need to protect the earth too. Your skin's health and the planet's health are connected. That's why we're serious about making sunscreen that's safe for reefs, good for the environment, and not tested on animals.

We stay away from stuff that harms the ocean, like oxybenzone and octinoxate. Instead, we use stuff that's clean and breaks down naturally, so it doesn't hurt coral reefs or sea life.

We try to use less plastic and make our packaging easy to recycle. We're always looking for ways to use stuff that can be refilled or composted. We also try to save energy and water when we make our products, and we make sure we get our ingredients in a good way. We believe that every little thing we do can make a big difference.

Our goal is simple: to make the world a better place than we found it.

🧬 Always Getting Better

We're always trying to make our products better. Our teams work with skin doctors, scientists, and environmental experts to make sure our sunscreen is safe, works well, and lasts long.

We do studies to see how our sunscreens protect your skin and how they feel over time. We check how they feel, how they soak in, and how they work in different weather and on different skin types. We want to make sunscreen that feels like you're not even wearing it – protection that's easy and feels good.

💛 For Everyone

We think everyone should be able to protect themselves from the sun. The sun shines on everyone, no matter what their skin color or background is. But sunscreen hasn't always been for everyone. Some sunscreens leave white marks, irritate sensitive skin, or don't work well for people with darker skin.

We've worked hard to fix that. Our sunscreens are clear, work for all skin types, and respect everyone's skin. Whether your skin is oily, dry, or sensitive, our products are made to work with you.

We also want to make sunscreen affordable. Everyone should be able to protect themselves, no matter how old they are, who they are, or how much money they make. That's why we help programs that teach people about sun protection and give sunscreen to schools, outdoor workers, and people who need it.

🧠 Teaching and Helping

We want to do more than just sell sunscreen – we want to teach people about skin health. A lot of people don't realize how much the sun can affect them all year round. We think that if people know more, they can prevent problems. We work with doctors and teachers to share information about how to choose, put on, and reapply sunscreen. We also tell people to wear hats and sunglasses and to stay out of the sun when it's strongest.

We hope to make sunscreen a normal thing that everyone does every day – a small thing that helps you stay healthy and feel good about yourself.
